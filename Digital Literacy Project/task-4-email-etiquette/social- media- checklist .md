**Social Media Checklist for College Students**



**Do’s**



* Maintain a professional and respectful tone in your posts and comments
* Think before posting—ensure your content is appropriate and meaningful
* Use privacy settings to control who can see your personal information
* Share educational, informative, or positive content that adds value
* Keep your profile updated with accurate and professional details
* Respect others’ opinions and engage in healthy discussions





&#x20;**Don’ts**

* Do not post offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content
* Avoid sharing personal information like phone number, address, or passwords
* Do not engage in cyberbullying, trolling, or arguments
* Avoid posting content that may harm your reputation or future career
* Do not accept friend requests from unknown or suspicious accounts
* Avoid spending excessive time on social media, affecting studies
