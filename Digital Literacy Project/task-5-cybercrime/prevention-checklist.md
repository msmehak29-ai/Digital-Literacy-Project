**Stay Safe Online – Checklist for College Student**





* Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts (avoid using the same password everywhere)
* Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on important apps like email and social media
* Do not click on unknown or suspicious links received via SMS, email, or social media
* Always check the website URL before entering personal information
* Keep your devices updated with the latest security updates and antivirus software
* Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive activities like logging into accounts
* Never share your UPI PIN or OTP, even with bank officials or customer care
* Always verify the receiver’s name before sending money via UPI apps





Report any cybercrime at the National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in

Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 (especially for financial fraud cases)
